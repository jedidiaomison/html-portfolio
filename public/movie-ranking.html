The Best Movies According to Jedidia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wind Rises

A beautifully animated historical drama with rich storytelling. It tells a story of a boy with big dreams of flying and designing airplanes. It showed us his personal ambitions, historical context, and the bittersweetness of human relationships.

The Tunnel to Summer, the Exit of Goodbyes

It has a visually stunning animation which showcases humanities tendency to sacrifice everything - even their lives - to fulfill their dreams and desires.

The Garden of Words

A unique and beautiful film that portrays the unexpected relationship between a student and a teacher. It explored the complexities of human relationships, particularly the challenges posed of age gap and the circumstances surrounding it.
