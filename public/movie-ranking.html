The Best Movies According to Jedidia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wind Rises

Great animation and rich storytelling. It is a story of a boy with big dreams of flying airplanes. It showed us his personal ambitions, history, and the bittersweetness of human relation ships.

The Tunnel to Summer, the Exit of Goodbyes

It has a visually stunning animation. It shows us the tendency of humans to sacrifice even their lives just to fulfill their dreams and desires.

The Garden of Words

It is a unique and beutiful movie that shows us the unexpected relationship between a student and a teacher. It explored a struggles of human relationships particularly the age gap and the circumstances revolving in it.
